簡介：

Studio A’ 成立於 2001 年 10 月，由 B+ 組織發起，A-dash 擔任主編曲。主要進行《仙境傳説》、KEY社 以及 Leaf社 等 GAME MUSIC 的同人改編。

社團音樂風格主要分為鋼琴與金屬。鋼琴方面，多使用簡單的和弦以突出旋律的抒情性，偶爾也使用柔和的氛圍音營造憂鬱感；金屬方面，則是透過富有感染力的實錄電吉他，並搭配合成器混音來創造強烈的情感張力。

2006年，Studio A’在發佈最後一張 CD 《TirNanorg 青空の下の日々》後，便逐漸因A-dash的淡出走向解散。2012 年，A-Dash 和 Namihei 組建了 ANastasia ，接管了 Studio A’ 的同人音樂活動。

2018年，隨著 Namihei 的淡出，ANastasia的同人活動也陷入停滯。事實上，ANastasia 在 2012 年起始便逐漸被 Dangerous Mezashi Cat 所吸收，2012 年末的《東方交戦劇》是最後記錄 ANastasia 的同人作品。

2019年，B+ 計劃復活 Studio A’，但由於内部和外部的雙重矛盾，StudioA’ Reboot 最終在年底徹底走向終結。

Studio A’ was founded in October 2001, with B+ as the organizer and A-dash as the main arranger. They primarily focused on creating doujin arrangements of game music from titles like Ragnarok Online, as well as games by KEY and Leaf.

The circle’s musical style can be broken down into two main categories: piano and metal. For their piano pieces, they often used simple chord progressions to highlight the lyrical melodies, occasionally incorporating soft ambient sounds to create a melancholic atmosphere. As for metal tracks, they achieved intense emotional impact through powerful live-recorded electric guitar parts combined with synthesizer mixing.

In 2006, following the release of their final CD “TirNanorg 青空の下の日々”, Studio A’ gradually dissolved due to A-dash’s withdrawal from the scene. In 2012, A-Dash and Namihei formed ANastasia, which took over Studio A’ is doujin music activities.

By 2018, ANastasia’s doujin activities stagnated following Namihei’s gradual departure. In fact, ANastasia had been progressively absorbed into Dangerous Mezashi Cat since its inception in 2012. The last appearance of the ANastasia name was in “東方冥異伝” (2023).
In 2019, B+ planned to revive Studio A’, but due to both internal conflicts and external pressures, StudioA’ Reboot ultimately came to a definitive end by the close of that year.
