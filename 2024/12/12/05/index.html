2006е№ҙпјҢStudio AвҖҷеңЁзҷјдҪҲжңҖеҫҢдёҖејө CD гҖҠTirNanorg йқ’з©әгҒ®дёӢгҒ®ж—ҘгҖ…гҖӢеҫҢпјҢдҫҝйҖҗжјёеӣ A-dashзҡ„ж·ЎеҮәиө°еҗ‘и§Јж•ЈгҖӮ2012 е№ҙпјҢA-Dash е’Ң Namihei зө„е»әдәҶ ANastasia пјҢжҺҘз®ЎдәҶ Studio AвҖҷ зҡ„еҗҢдәәйҹіжЁӮжҙ»еӢ•гҖӮ

2018е№ҙпјҢйҡЁи‘— Namihei зҡ„ж·ЎеҮәпјҢANastasiaзҡ„еҗҢдәәжҙ»еӢ•д№ҹйҷ·е…ҘеҒңж»ҜгҖӮдәӢеҜҰдёҠпјҢANastasia еңЁ 2012 е№ҙиө·е§ӢдҫҝйҖҗжјёиў« Dangerous Mezashi Cat жүҖеҗёж”¶пјҢ2012 е№ҙжң«зҡ„гҖҠжқұж–№дәӨжҲҰеҠҮгҖӢжҳҜжңҖеҫҢиЁҳйҢ„ ANastasia зҡ„еҗҢдәәдҪңе“ҒгҖӮ

2019е№ҙпјҢB+ иЁҲеҠғеҫ©жҙ» Studio AвҖҷпјҢдҪҶз”ұж–јеҶ…йғЁе’ҢеӨ–йғЁзҡ„йӣҷйҮҚзҹӣзӣҫпјҢStudioAвҖҷ Reboot жңҖзөӮеңЁе№ҙеә•еҫ№еә•иө°еҗ‘зөӮзөҗгҖӮ

Studio AвҖҷ was founded in October 2001, with B+ as the organizer and A-dash as the main arranger. They primarily focused on creating doujin arrangements of game music from titles like Ragnarok Online, as well as games by KEY and Leaf.

The circleвҖҷs musical style can be broken down into two main categories: piano and metal. For their piano pieces, they often used simple chord progressions to highlight the lyrical melodies, occasionally incorporating soft ambient sounds to create a melancholic atmosphere. As for metal tracks, they achieved intense emotional impact through powerful live-recorded electric guitar parts combined with synthesizer mixing.

In 2006, following the release of their final CD вҖңTirNanorg йқ’з©әгҒ®дёӢгҒ®ж—ҘгҖ…вҖқ, Studio AвҖҷ gradually dissolved due to A-dashвҖҷs withdrawal from the scene. In 2012, A-Dash and Namihei formed ANastasia, which took over Studio AвҖҷ is doujin music activities.

By 2018, ANastasiaвҖҷs doujin activities stagnated following NamiheiвҖҷs gradual departure. In fact, ANastasia had been progressively absorbed into Dangerous Mezashi Cat since its inception in 2012. The last appearance of the ANastasia name was in вҖңжқұж–№еҶҘз•°дјқвҖқ (2023).
In 2019, B+ planned to revive Studio AвҖҷ, but due to both internal conflicts and external pressures, StudioAвҖҷ Reboot ultimately came to a definitive end by the close of that year.
